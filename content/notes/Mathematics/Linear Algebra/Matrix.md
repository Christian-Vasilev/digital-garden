The [[notes/Mathematics/Linear Algebra/Linear systems]]

$$
\begin{align}
3x + 2y = 7 \\
x - 6y = 0
\end{align}
$$

can be represented as a matrix.

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
3 & 2 \\
1 & -6
\end{bmatrix}
$$

By bringing the constants of the equation, we get the **augmented matrix** (разширена матрица):

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
3 & 2 &\bigm| & 7\\ 
1 & -6 &\bigm| & 0 
\end{bmatrix}
$$
