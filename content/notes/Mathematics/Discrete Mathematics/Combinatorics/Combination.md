The combination is selection of elements without repetition from a collection **when order doesn't matter**.


Without repetition:

$$
C(n, r) = \frac{n!}{r!\cdot(n-r)!}
$$

With repetition:

$$
C(n, r) = \frac{(r+n-1)!}{r!\cdot(n-1)!}
